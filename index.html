About

Who I Am

I'm a game designer, developer, and 3D animator based in California. I graduated from the University of Texas at Austin in May 2025 with a B.S. in Art & Entertainment Technologies (GPA: 3.8), concentrating in game development.

I'm currently leading development at Digital Wizards Studios on a commercial iOS app — serving as the sole game development authority on the project, translating client vision into gameplay systems and shipping features end-to-end, from Unity scripting to Xcode deployment.

I find every layer of game development compelling: the design logic of systems, the craft of animation, the problem-solving of programming, and the storytelling that ties it together. Outside of games, I produce video content and have a soft spot for open-world exploration and turn-based RPGs.

I'm actively looking for roles in game design, game development, or 3D animation starting this fall.
